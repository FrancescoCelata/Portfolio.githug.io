The Route Back is a game created during the Global Game Jam 2022. It was the first real group project I participated in that led to the creation of a complete (albeit problem-filled) game. It was a unique experience that I will always cherish, both for the wonderful people I collaborated with and for the lessons I learned.


Starting with a game jam was certainly a particularly challenging step to take, but there couldn't have been a better experience to understand right away what it takes to create video games.
