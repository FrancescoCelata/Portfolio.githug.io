I'm an italian Game Designer born in 2002 in Rome.

From 2022 to 2025, I studied at AIV where, in addition to receiving lessons from active Game Designers and Level Designers in the gaming industry, I participated in numerous group projects focused on the development and publication of small projects.

More importantly, I learned to work in teams, applying work organization techniques such as the Agile Method and simulating a professional work environment.

Growing up surrounded by video games (my father worked for Computer Union until 2001), and nurtured by my family's affinity for music, technology and programming, cinema and videogames, I've embarked on this path fueled by both heritage and personal fascination.

Initially, I decided to pursue a career in the video game industry, captivated by the opportunity to fully unleash my creativity, inspired by the great works of art that shaped me.

What truly captivated me, however, is the collaborative spirit that permeates the game development process. A camaraderie not merely driven by academic tasks but by the shared pursuit of a collective dream.
